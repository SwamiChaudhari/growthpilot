# LEAD SCORING METHODOLOGY

Each business scored 1-100 across 5 dimensions:

1. BUDGET POTENTIAL (25 pts) - Can they afford a professional website?
   - 20-25: High revenue business (medical, real estate, hospitality)
   - 12-19: Medium revenue (coaching, events, photography)
   - 5-11: Lower revenue (salons, repair shops)

2. WEBSITE NEED (25 pts) - How badly do they need a website?
   - 20-25: No website at all
   - 12-19: Outdated/poor mobile experience
   - 5-11: Has basic website but needs upgrade

3. LEAD GENERATION POTENTIAL (20 pts) - Can website generate measurable revenue?
   - 16-20: Direct booking/appointment businesses
   - 8-15: Inquiry-based businesses
   - 3-7: Informational only

4. LOCAL COMPETITION (15 pts) - Do competitors have stronger websites?
   - 12-15: Competitors have strong sites, they don't
   - 6-11: Mixed competitive landscape
   - 1-5: No strong competitors online

5. DECISION-MAKER ACCESSIBILITY (15 pts) - Can owner be contacted easily?
   - 12-15: Owner active on Google Maps, responds to reviews
   - 6-11: Business has contact info available
   - 1-5: Hard to reach decision maker
